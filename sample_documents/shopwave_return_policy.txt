SHOPWAVE — RETURNS, REFUNDS & EXCHANGE POLICY
Effective: 1 January 2026

1. RETURN WINDOW
Most products may be returned within 14 days of delivery for a full refund or
replacement. Electronics and large appliances have a 7-day return window.
Perishable items (Food & Snacks) and beauty products with broken seals are
non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

2. ELIGIBILITY
To be eligible, the item must be:
  - Unused and in the same condition as received
  - In its original packaging with all tags, manuals, and accessories
  - Accompanied by the original invoice or order ID

3. HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
  a. Open My Orders in your ShopWave account
  b. Select the order and click "Return / Exchange"
  c. Choose a reason and upload a clear photo of the item
  d. A pickup will be scheduled within 48 hours

4. REFUNDS
Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days
after the returned item passes quality check at our warehouse. UPI and wallet
refunds are typically faster (1-3 days) than card refunds (5-7 days).
For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are issued as ShopWave store credit or
to a bank account you specify in the return form.

5. EXCHANGES
Size or colour exchanges are free for Fashion items if the replacement is in
stock. Out-of-stock exchanges are converted to a refund automatically.

6. DAMAGED OR WRONG ITEM RECEIVED
Report damaged, defective, or incorrectly shipped items within 48 hours of
delivery via the in-app chat or by emailing care@shopwave.example. Include
photos of the packaging and the item. Replacement is shipped at no cost.

7. NON-RETURNABLE CATEGORIES
  - Innerwear and swimwear
  - Customised or personalised products
  - Gift cards and digital codes
  - Items marked "Final Sale" on the product page
