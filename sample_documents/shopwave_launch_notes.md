# ShopWave 2026 Spring Launch — Internal Brief

**Owner:** Product Marketing
**Status:** Draft v3
**Launch date:** 15 March 2026

## Executive summary

ShopWave's Spring 2026 catalogue introduces 84 new SKUs across six categories,
anchored by a refreshed Electronics line and a co-branded Fashion capsule.
Target: ₹4.2 Cr GMV in the first 30 days, +28% YoY against Spring 2025.

## Hero launches

### Electronics
- **Aurora Buds Pro 3** — ANC earbuds with 36hr battery, ₹4,999 (₹6,499 MRP)
- **Helix K-Pad Mini** — 65% mechanical keyboard, hot-swappable, ₹3,299
- **Nova Watch S2** — AMOLED smartwatch, AOD, GPS, ₹5,499

### Fashion
- **Tide x ShopWave** — limited-run sneaker capsule, 4 colourways, 1,200 units
- **Studio Linen** — breathable summer dresses, sizes XS-XXL, ₹1,799-2,499

### Home & Living
- **Ember Stone** — soy-wax candles in 6 scents, ₹449 each
- **Brisk K1** — 1.7L variable-temp electric kettle, ₹2,199

## Pricing strategy

| Tier        | Discount band | Categories                          |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------------------------|
| Hero        | 20-30% off    | Electronics, Fashion capsule        |
| Everyday    | 10-15% off    | Home, Beauty, Sports                |
| Flash sale  | up to 60% off | Rotating SKUs, 6-hour windows       |

All prices listed in Indian Rupees (INR). The Flash Sale countdown timer on
the Deals page updates every second and resets at 00:00 IST daily.

## Marketing checkpoints

- **Wk -2:** Email teaser to top 10% LTV customers
- **Wk -1:** Influencer seeding (12 creators, mix of Fashion + Tech)
- **Launch day:** Homepage takeover, push notification, paid social burst
- **Wk +1:** Performance review, restock decisions, inventory rebalance

## Risks

1. **Inventory:** Tide x ShopWave capsule is single-batch; sellout expected
   within 72 hours. No restock planned.
2. **Logistics:** South India warehouse renovation may delay Bangalore
   orders by 1-2 days during launch week.
3. **FX:** Component costs in USD/CNY rose 6% QoQ — Q2 margin under pressure
   if INR weakens further.
